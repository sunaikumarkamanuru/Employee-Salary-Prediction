About Employee Salary Prediction

Employee Salary Prediction is a machine learning web application designed to estimate employee salaries based on professional experience, education level, skills, certifications, industry, company size, and work location.

The application uses a trained machine learning model built with Python and Scikit-Learn to provide salary estimates based on user inputs.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide students, job seekers, professionals, and recruiters with insights into potential salary ranges using data-driven predictions.

Disclaimer

Salary predictions are estimates only and should not be considered financial, legal, employment, or career advice. Actual salaries may vary based on market conditions, company policies, geography, and other factors.
